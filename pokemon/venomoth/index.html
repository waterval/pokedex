Biography

Venomoth is a Poison Moth Pokémon. It has 1890 health. After trying to catch it with a Pokéball, there is a 7% chance it will flee. Venomoth has a minimum length of 1.31m and can grow up to 1.69m. It weighs between 10.94kg and 14.06kg. Venomoth has both the Bug and the Poison type.
